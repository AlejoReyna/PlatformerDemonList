Do levels adhere to standards?

Rated levels automatically make the list, but for unrated levels, there's an assessment process. The list actively discourages spam-oriented levels, so levels must adhere to a 10 clicks-per-second cap. This applies to a single input; for example, 6 clicks per second on two keys simultaneously (such as left + right) is still acceptable, even though it totals 12 clicks per second cumulatively. Levels relying on random triggers or employing "luck" for difficulty would also be disqualified in most cases.

Prohibited hacks/exploits

Here's a current list of unacceptable hacks that will result in your record being rejected:

  • Noclip
  • Speed hacks
  • Show hitboxes
  • Hitbox multiplier
  • Show layout
  • No spikes
  • No hitboxes
  • Show trajectory
  • Bots/Macros
  • Pause countdown
  • Nerfing levels
  • Frame stepper
  • No mirror
  • No rotation
  • Swiftclick shortcut
  • Instant complete
  • Autoing

Miscellaneous levels

Various levels like boss fights/minigames will have their own distinct categories since they differ significantly from typical platformers. This differentiation aims to prevent inaccuracies and difficulty in ordering the list.

How do I submit a completion?

Submissions are made through our Discord server, and you must include raw footage with recorded clicks. If the level has checkpoints, you can also upload a spliced completion, but raw footage must be shared upon request.
